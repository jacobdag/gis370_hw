State Voter Turnout/Ballots counted (2020 Primary Election)

I chose to map the percentage of people (by state) that voted in the 2020 General Election. I was interested in this because most of the election data that I see visually represented is that of party affiliation, and what states, or parts of states, voted Democrat or Republican. I think that voter turnout is important for our democracy, making it important to track and present.

I used a natural breaks categorization to get the best groupings. The other methods resulted in a decent amount states being placed just outside of groups, and the real trends were not clearly portrayed.
